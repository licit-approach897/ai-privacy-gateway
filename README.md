# 🛡️ ai-privacy-gateway - Protect your AI data from leaks

Your AI tools send data to servers across the globe. Often, this data includes private information. The ai-privacy-gateway acts as a shield between your computer and AI services like ChatGPT, Claude, or Cursor. It masks sensitive details before they leave your device.

## 📋 What this tool does

Modern AI tools need your data to work. However, you might share passwords, client names, or financial records by mistake. This tool intercepts your outgoing traffic. It removes private data while keeping the rest of your prompt intact. It ensures your secrets stay on your machine.

## ⚙️ How to use the privacy gateway

The gateway runs in the background. It sits between your applications and the internet.

1. Open the application. You will see a green light when the shield is active.
2. Configure your AI application settings. Change the proxy address to `localhost` and set the port to `8080`.
3. The gateway now screens your traffic.
4. You can check the dashboard to see which items the tool blocked.

## 🛡️ Privacy features

- **Data Masking:** Hides patterns like credit card numbers.
- **Redaction:** Removes names and physical addresses.
- **Local Processing:** All screening happens on your machine. No data goes to our servers.
- **Real-time Monitoring:** Blocks risky requests before they reach the AI provider.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

- **Application fails to open:** Restart your computer and try again. Ensure no other proxy software competes for the same network port.
- **AI application reports a connection error:** Verify that the gateway is running. Confirm your port settings match the configuration in the gateway settings menu.
- **Important sites stop working:** Add those sites to the bypass list in the settings menu.

## 🔒 Your data stays yours

This tool uses a local scanning engine. It examines the text you send to AI services. If it finds a match for sensitive strings, it replaces the content with a placeholder. The AI receives the context it needs without seeing your private details.

Yes. You can route multiple applications through the same gateway.

No. The processing occurs locally on your computer.
